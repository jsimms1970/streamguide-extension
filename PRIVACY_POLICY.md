# Privacy Policy for StreamGuide - Where to Watch

**Last updated: December 28, 2025**

## Overview

StreamGuide - Where to Watch is a browser extension that helps you find where to stream movies and TV shows. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **None.** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

### What the Extension Does
- Reads the title of movies/TV shows from supported websites (IMDB, Rotten Tomatoes, Google)
- Sends the title to our public API (streamguide-api.onrender.com) to fetch streaming availability
- Stores your widget visibility preferences locally in your browser (localStorage)

### What We Don't Do
- We don't collect personal information
- We don't track your browsing history
- We don't use cookies
- We don't sell or share any data
- We don't require account creation

## Third-Party Services

The extension communicates with:
- **StreamGuide API** (streamguide-api.onrender.com) - To fetch streaming availability data
- No other third-party services are used

## Local Storage

The extension uses your browser's localStorage to remember:
- Whether you've closed the widget on specific pages

This data never leaves your browser.

## Permissions Explained

- **storage**: To save your widget preferences locally
- **activeTab**: To read the current page title and show relevant streaming info
- **scripting**: To restore the widget when you click "Show Widget"
- **Host permissions**: To communicate with our API and inject the widget on supported sites

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.
